✓ Critical Principles
→Launch utility first, token second — utility must precede token value
→Real-world revenue must back buybacks from Day 1 of operations
→Community + Treasury must equal 50% minimum for RWA models
→Maintain 4-year team vesting — non-negotiable for VC trust
→Staking rewards must be tied to usage, not passive farming
→All vesting via audited smart contracts — fully transparent on-chain
✕ Fatal Mistakes to Avoid
→Never launch token before core utility is live and generating revenue
→Never over-allocate to insiders — red flag for community and VCs
→Never release >15% supply at TGE — volatility destroys price discovery
→Never rely solely on rewards to sustain demand — real utility required
→Never allow large investor cliff unlocks without price support mechanism
→Never use ETH/stablecoins for core payments — kills value capture loop
