→Use the mark on clean, uncluttered backgrounds only
→Maintain clearspace equal to the gold seed dot's diameter
→Scale the logo proportionally at all times
→Use only approved color combinations from this brand kit
→Always pair the mark with Poppins typeface
✕ Don't
→Distort, stretch, rotate, or crop the logo mark
