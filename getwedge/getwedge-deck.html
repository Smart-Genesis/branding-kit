07What You Get

Not data. Decisions.

Every signal Wedge surfaces comes with a headline, an interpretation, and a recommended action. Three sample insights from a typical week.

IMPORTANCE 9 HIRING · MAY 12

Linear is hiring 4 AI engineers — fast.

Four senior ML / AI roles posted in the last 14 days, all focused on "intelligent issue routing" and "predictive triage." This is a major bet on AI-native workflow, not just AI features.

▲ Recommended Action Decide in the next 30 days whether AI workflow is core to your roadmap or a deliberate non-bet. Either is fine — drifting is not.
IMPORTANCE 7 REVIEWS · MAY 10

Jira import speed is hurting 3 competitors.

17 G2 and Reddit complaints in 30 days specifically about slow imports from Jira. Affects ClickUp, Asana, and Monday equally. The pattern is consistent enough to be a positioning angle.

▲ Recommended Action If your import is faster, lead with it on the landing page. If it isn't, this is a 2-week engineering investment that opens a real wedge.
IMPORTANCE 6 PRICING · MAY 8

Notion quietly raised Team prices 18%.

Team tier moved from $10 to $11.80 per seat last week. No announcement, no email. They're testing whether sticker shock or churn responds first.

▲ Recommended Action Run the math on your own pricing this quarter. The market just gave you cover to test a 10–15% increase without standing out.

Each insight links to its source. Each recommended action is concrete. Nothing is "consider exploring opportunities to leverage."
